# Shipping Policy

## Overview

We ship to addresses within the country and selected international destinations. Orders are processed on business days (Monday–Friday, excluding public holidays).

## Processing Time

All orders go through a brief processing period before being handed to the carrier:

- Standard orders: 1–2 business days to process
- Orders placed on weekends or public holidays begin processing the next business day

You will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number once your order has been dispatched.

## Shipping Options and Delivery Times

We offer the following shipping methods at checkout:

| Method | Estimated Delivery | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Standard Shipping | 5–7 business days | $4.99 (free over $50) |
| Express Shipping | 2–3 business days | $12.99 |
| Overnight Shipping | Next business day | $24.99 |

Delivery estimates begin from the date of dispatch, not the date the order is placed.

## Free Shipping

Standard shipping is free on all orders with a subtotal of $50.00 or more (after any discounts are applied). Express and Overnight options are always charged at the rates above, regardless of order value.

## Carriers

We use a mix of carriers depending on your location and the shipping method selected:

- Domestic: UPS, FedEx, USPS
- International: DHL, FedEx International

The specific carrier for your shipment is shown in your shipping confirmation email.

## Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email containing:

- Your tracking number
- A direct link to the carrier's tracking page
- The estimated delivery date

You can also check your order status at any time by contacting our support team with your order number and the email address used at checkout. Tracking information may take up to 24 hours to update after dispatch.

## International Shipping

We currently ship to selected countries. International orders may be subject to:

- Import duties, taxes, and customs fees charged by the destination country
- Longer delivery times (typically 7–21 business days depending on destination and customs clearance)

These additional charges are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in the price you pay at checkout. We recommend checking your country's import regulations before ordering.

We do not ship to P.O. boxes for express or overnight orders. Standard shipping to P.O. boxes is available for domestic orders only.

## Lost or Missing Packages

If your tracking information shows the package as delivered but you have not received it:

1. Check around your delivery location and with neighbors or building reception.
2. Wait 24 hours — carriers sometimes mark packages as delivered slightly early.
3. If still missing after 24 hours, contact our support team with your order number. We will open an investigation with the carrier.

If a package is confirmed lost by the carrier, we will reship your order or issue a full refund at your preference.

## Undeliverable Packages

If a package is returned to us due to an incorrect address or failed delivery attempts, we will contact you to arrange reshipment. Reshipment fees may apply for address corrections. If you do not respond within 14 days, the order will be refunded minus the original shipping cost.

## Questions

For any shipping questions, contact our support team via the chat widget with your order number ready.
