# Payment FAQ

## What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

- **Credit and debit cards**: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- **PayPal**: Pay with your PayPal balance, linked bank account, or PayPal credit
- **Apple Pay** and **Google Pay**: Available on supported browsers and devices
- **Store credit**: Applied automatically if you have a balance on your account

We do not currently accept bank transfers, checks, cryptocurrency, or cash on delivery.

## When will my card be charged?

Your card is charged at the time your order is placed, not when it ships. If your order is cancelled before shipment, a full refund is issued immediately, though it may take 3–10 business days to appear on your statement depending on your bank.

## Why was my payment declined?

Common reasons for a declined payment include:

- Incorrect card number, expiry date, or CVV
- Billing address does not match what your bank has on file
- Insufficient funds or credit limit reached
- Your bank has flagged the transaction as suspicious

If your card is declined, please double-check the details you entered, then try again or use a different payment method. If the problem persists, contact your bank directly — we are unable to see the specific reason a bank declines a transaction.

## Is my payment information secure?

Yes. We do not store your full card number on our servers. All payment processing is handled by PCI-DSS compliant payment processors (Stripe and PayPal). Your card details are encrypted in transit using TLS.

## How do refunds work?

When a refund is approved (either for a return or an order cancellation):

- The refund is issued to the original payment method used at checkout.
- **Credit/debit card refunds**: 5–10 business days to appear on your statement, depending on your card issuer.
- **PayPal refunds**: Typically within 3–5 business days to your PayPal balance.
- **Store credit refunds**: Applied to your account immediately and available for use on your next order.

We cannot issue refunds to a different payment method than the one used for the original purchase.

## What is store credit and how do I use it?

Store credit is issued in cases such as goodwill gestures, certain return resolutions, or promotional rewards. Your store credit balance is visible in your account settings.

Store credit is applied automatically at checkout if you have a balance — you will see it deducted before the final total. Store credit does not expire and can be combined with other discounts.

## Can I change my payment method after placing an order?

No. Once an order is placed, the payment method cannot be changed. If you need to use a different payment method, you would need to cancel the existing order (if it has not yet shipped) and place a new one.

## Do you charge sales tax?

Sales tax is calculated at checkout based on your shipping address and applicable local tax laws. The tax amount is shown in your order summary before you confirm payment.

## Can I split payment across multiple methods?

You can apply store credit alongside another payment method (card or PayPal). However, we do not support splitting payment across two different cards or between PayPal and a card simultaneously.

## What happens if I am charged twice?

Duplicate charges are rare and usually result from a browser or network issue during checkout. If you believe you have been charged twice for the same order, please contact our support team with your order number. We will investigate and issue a refund for any duplicate charge within 1–2 business days.

## How do I update my saved payment information?

You can manage saved payment methods in your account settings under "Payment Methods." Changes take effect on your next order — they do not affect any orders already placed.

## Questions?

If you have a payment question not answered here, contact our support team via the chat widget. Please have your order number ready if your question relates to a specific order.
