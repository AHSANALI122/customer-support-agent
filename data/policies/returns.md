# Return Policy

## Overview

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy with an item, you may return it within 30 days of the delivery date for a full refund or exchange.

## Eligibility

To qualify for a return, items must meet all of the following conditions:

- The return is initiated within 30 days of the delivery date
- The item is unused and in its original condition
- The item is in its original packaging with all tags, accessories, and documentation included
- You have proof of purchase (order number or order confirmation email)

Items that show signs of use, damage caused by the customer, or are missing original packaging may be subject to a partial refund or refused entirely.

## Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

- Digital products and software licenses (once activated or downloaded)
- Perishable goods
- Personalized or custom-made items
- Hazardous materials
- Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable" on the product page

## How to Initiate a Return

1. Contact our support team with your order number and the reason for the return. You can do this via the chat widget or by emailing support.
2. Our team will review your request and, if approved, send you a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number along with return shipping instructions.
3. Pack the item securely in its original packaging. Write your RMA number on the outside of the package.
4. Ship the item to the address provided in the return instructions.

Do not ship items back without first receiving an RMA number — unreturned packages may be refused or lost.

## Return Shipping Costs

- If the return is due to our error (wrong item shipped, item arrived damaged or defective), we will cover the return shipping cost and provide a prepaid shipping label.
- For all other returns (change of mind, ordered the wrong size, etc.), the customer is responsible for return shipping costs.

## Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will notify you by email. If the return is approved:

- Refunds are issued to the original payment method used at checkout.
- Processing takes 3–5 business days after we receive the item.
- Depending on your bank or card issuer, it may take an additional 5–10 business days for the refund to appear on your statement.

If the return is rejected (item does not meet eligibility criteria), we will notify you and return the item to you at your cost.

## Exchanges

If you would like to exchange an item for a different size or variant, please initiate a return as described above and place a new order for the correct item. This ensures the fastest turnaround, as we cannot hold inventory during the return process.

## Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrived damaged or is defective, please contact us within 7 days of delivery. Include photos of the damage in your message. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you, including return shipping.

## Questions

If you have any questions about your return or refund, our support team is available via the chat widget. Please have your order number ready to help us assist you faster.
